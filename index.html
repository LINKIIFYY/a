About this Application

The CIB Mobile Banking app is designed to meet all your banking needs quickly and with ease!
Now you can access CIB banking services on the go and manage your finances anytime and anywhere!

• Register for Mobile Banking using the method that’s most convenient to you (Account number, passport number, national ID number, debit card number, or credit card number)
• View account balances, credit cards, deposits, loans, and mutual funds
• Easily transfer money to anyone, anywhere, and make donations. You can also schedule one-time or recurring transactions
• Pay and settle your or someone else’s CIB credit card
• Book a certificate of deposit or time deposit, and open additional accounts
• Submit new checkbook requests, block lost or stolen credit cards, dispute credit card transactions, subscribe to e-statements, replace your cards, apply for a loan and a credit card, etc.
• Activate your OTP token
• Use the currency calculator
• Find the nearest CIB ATM and branch to you
• And countless other services designed to make your life and banking experience easier!
